# OpenIslam Wiki

OpenIslam Wiki publishes canonical long-form articles and offers alternate ways to read the same material without creating competing documents.

## Reading Deck Language

**Reading Deck**:
The alternate full-screen reading surface attached to a canonical article.
_Avoid_: Slide deck, presentation

**Reading Feed**:
An ordered sequence of Cards sourced from either the canonical article or its separately authored TLDR.
_Avoid_: Deck mode, slide list

**Quick Read**:
The Reading Feed sourced from the separately authored TLDR.
_Avoid_: Summary mode, TLDR slides

**Deep Read**:
The Reading Feed derived from the complete canonical article.
_Avoid_: Full article mode, article slides

**Card**:
One navigable portion of a Reading Feed.
_Avoid_: Slide

**Sources Card**:
The final counted Card containing the Reading Feed's references and notes.
_Avoid_: Bibliography slide

**Completion State**:
The terminal state reached by advancing beyond the Sources Card. It is not a Card and is excluded from contents, progress, and Card totals.
_Avoid_: Outro slide, completion card
