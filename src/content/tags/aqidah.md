Aqidah — creed — is the foundation from which all else follows. It concerns the most fundamental questions a person can ask: who or what is God, what is the nature of creation, how do we know anything about the unseen?

These posts engage those questions through the lens of classical Sunni theology, examining the textual evidence and the reasoning that has guided Muslim scholars across centuries.
