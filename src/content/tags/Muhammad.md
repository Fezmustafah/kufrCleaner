**
No figure in history has been more written about, more debated, or more misrepresented than Muhammad ﷺ. The sources on his life are extensive — the Quran, the Hadith collections, the Seerah literature — and the picture they paint is of a man of extraordinary moral depth.

These posts look at different facets of that life: his mercy, his role as husband and father, the miracles attributed to him, and what non-Muslim scholars across the centuries have said when they engaged his biography honestly.
