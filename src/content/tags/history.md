History is not a passive record — it is an argument about what happened and why it matters. These posts engage with Islamic history on its own terms: reading sources carefully, weighing competing accounts, and resisting the temptation to flatten a rich past into a simple story.

From the golden age of Islamic scholarship to the contested historiography of slavery, the goal is the same — honest engagement with what the record actually says.
