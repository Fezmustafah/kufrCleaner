The academic study of Islam has produced both illuminating scholarship and deeply distorted portraits. These posts take both seriously — citing research where it is reliable, and pushing back where methodology or assumptions lead scholars astray.

Engaging academia honestly means neither uncritical deference nor reflexive dismissal. It means reading carefully, following citations, and asking what the evidence actually supports.
