The Seerah — the biography of the Prophet ﷺ — is not merely a historical record. It is a living source of guidance, studied by Muslims across fourteen centuries to understand how Islam is meant to be lived. These posts approach that study seriously: drawing on classical scholarship, examining primary sources, and situating events in their historical context.
